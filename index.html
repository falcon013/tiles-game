1. Board has two card of matching colors. You see back side of the card.

1. Choose one card on the board, it flip to shows the color.

2. Choose second card to guess the matching color.

3. If both chosen cards are a matching color, they disappear from the board.

4. If the cards are not a match they are turned back over and it is now the next turn.

5. The game continues until no cards left to open.
